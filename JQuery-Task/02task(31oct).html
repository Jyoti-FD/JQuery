BTS


BTS was formed in 2012 after a series of auditions held by BigHit. The seven-member K-pop group -- which consists of RM, V, Jung
Kook, Jin, Suga, Jimin and J-Hope -- made its debut on June 13, 2013, with '2 Cool 4 Skool.' The septet developed a loyal worldwide
following called ARMY, with 'The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 2' -- which was released in November 2015 -- becoming BTS' first
album to debut on the Billboard 200, arriving at No. 171 on the Dec. 19, 2015-dated chart. When 'Love Yourself: Tear' was released in
May 2018, it debuted at No. 1 on the all-genre tally, making the album not only BTS' first Billboard 200 chart-topper, but also the
first K-pop album to reach the summit. Since then, BTS has garnered multiple Grammy nominations, including best pop duo/group
performance ("Dynamite," "Butter" and "My Universe") and best music video ("Yet to Come"). The group has also had multiple cross-genre
collaborations, including with Benny Blanco and Snoop Dogg ("Bad Decisions"), Halsey ("Boy With Luv"), Coldplay ("My
Universe"), Megan Thee Stallion ("Butter" remix), Nicki Minaj ("Idol") and many more.



You Never Walk Alone and Love Yourself


In February 2017, BTS released the repackaged edition of Wings entitled You Never Walk Alone. The 700,000 pre-orders of it (an
increase from the 500,000 pre-orders of Wings) helped break the record for most albums sold in a month in South Korea, as it
reached 1.49 million copies by the end of its first month. The lead single was "Spring Day" and it won Best Song of the Year at the
2017 Melon Music Awards. BTS's second world tour, 2017 BTS Live Trilogy Episode III: The Wings Tour, began in February. On the
tour, BTS played arenas in the US, such as New Jersey's Prudential Center and California's Honda Center. Tickets for the North
American leg sold out within hours and two shows were added. After completing the North American leg, BTS attended the 24th
Billboard Music Awards in May and won Top Social Artist, the first K-pop group to win a Billboard award.[82] BTS fans cast over 300
million votes for the band and broke a six-year winning streak held by Justin Bieber, a performer with 100 million Twitter
followers. This caused the international media to focus on the ability of BTS's fandom to propel the group to such a victory.



Hiking


If you have friends who hike, ask them to take you on a trek. Most people are happy to share their expertise, let you borrow gear
and introduce newbies to their favorite trails. If you don’t know any hikers, many cities and towns have hiking clubs that regularly plan outings.
You can find hiking classes, outings and events through REI Experiences. Online groups are also a great way to find hiking buddies.


As a beginner hiker, it can be tough to know how much food and water you need, A good general recommendation for how much to eat is
200–300 calories per hour. For water intake, about a half liter per hour of moderate activity in moderate temperatures is a good
starting place. These amounts depend heavily on several factors, such as the intensity of your hike, the weather, your age, your sweat
rate and your body type. As you gain more experience, you’ll get a better sense for just how much you need.
